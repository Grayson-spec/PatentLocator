About Us

Do you have a million-dollar idea but aren't sure if it's already been featured on Shark Tank?

Well, we're here to help! Using Patent Finder, we take the stress away from any worries about

patent infringement. No more concerns about being hunted down or receiving pesky cease-and-desist

letters. With integrated APIs from sources like Google Patents, USPTO, and WIPO, we've compiled

over 10 million registered patents worldwide. That way, you can move forward confidently, knowing

your idea is legally protected.
